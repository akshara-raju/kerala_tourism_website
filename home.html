This is why you should Travel

HOUSE BOATS

These dot the frequently mentioned backwaters of the region. You will be surprised when you enter these slowly moving barges. They offer all the comforts of a hotel room, if not more!

DANCE FORMS

Kerala is a culturally rich state. It is also the birthplace of one of the most popular dance forms from India, the ‘Kathakali’. Another mesmerizing dance form is the ‘Theyyam’, which is very popular in Northern Kerala.There are plenty more dance forms too. You should certainly see these wherever and whenever you get a chance because they are a visual treat.

KALARIPAYATTU

Kerala is also known for the ‘mother of all martial arts’, ‘Kalaripayattu’. This martial art is considered to be the precursor to kungfu and is one of the oldest.

BEACHES

Kerala beaches are amongst the best in India. Some of the beaches remain relatively deserted. Yet, in case you don't want to relax, you'll find plenty going on at others. Also Muzhappilangad drive-in is considered as the longest drive-in beach in Asia.

ARCHITECTURE

Kerala is known for its unique architecture too. The style of architecture found in this state differs from the rest of Southern India. The architecture reflects Kerala’s rich cultural history and features medieval patterns.

CUISINE

Kerala cuisine has an abundance of coconut, rice, tapioca and spices like black pepper, cloves, cinnamon and ginger. The Portuguese introduced cassava, now widely eaten in Kerala. The region is also famous for its Sadhya, served at the Hindu festival Onam and consisting of boiled rice and a host of vegetarian dishes on a banana leaf. Kerala cuisine also features a lot of sea food like fish, prawns, mussels and crabs because of its long coastline.

AYURVEDIC TREATMENTS

Kerala is probably the only place in the entire world where Ayurveda is used as a mainstream method of treatment. The ‘Panchakarma’ is a popular Ayurvedic treatment that uses medicated oil, milk and herbs.

TEA AND COFFEE PLANTATIONS

The sprawling tea and coffee plantations with a delicious fragrance wafting in the air are one of the highlights of Kerala. The regions of Munnar, Kochi and Periyar are replete with these plantations. You can take a mesmerising drive through these gardens and you will get a slice of peace and beauty. These gardens are extremely picturesque and have served as backdrops for various mainstream films!

VALLAM KALI

Vallam Kali, a place here is renowned for the traditional boat race in Kerala. This boat race is conducted during the festival of Onam, the Annual Harvest Festival of Kerala. The best time to visit Kerala to enjoy this Snake Boat Race is from August to October.
